    • Chocolate Coconut Cake Recipe

    Introduction

    Easy to make, dense chocolate cake, with a surprise coconut flavor

    Ingredients

    Ingredients
    Qty Unit Ingredient
    1 cup flower
    0.5 cup cocoa
    0.5 cup Shokolit
    0.75 cup sugar
    0.5 cup oil
    1 cup water
    1 pack vanilla sugar
    1 cup coconut
    2 eggs

    Instruction

    Mixing

    First we need to mix all the dry ingredients: flower, cocoa, Shokolit, vanilla shugar and shugar. After all the ingredients are combined, add in the water, oil and eggs (one at a time).

    The Surprise

    Add in the coconut and mix untill the mixture is bind and thick.

    Baking

    Heat an oven to 180 degrees Celcius. When warm enough, put the cake mixture in to tins and set it in the oven for about 25 minutes. Stick in a toothpick to check, when it comes out dry - the cake is ready!
    Add any toppings you want, and enjoy! :)
